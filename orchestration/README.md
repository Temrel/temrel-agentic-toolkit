# Orchestration and multi-agent workflows

One of the six elements of agentic engineering. This is about **coordinating more than one step or more than one agent**: pipelines, fan-out and fan-in, planner and worker splits, retries, and the control flow that decides who does what and in what order. Good orchestration turns a single clever agent into a reliable system.

## What belongs here

Multi-agent and workflow helpers: orchestration scripts, agent-coordination patterns, planner and worker templates, queue and retry utilities, and reusable workflow definitions.
