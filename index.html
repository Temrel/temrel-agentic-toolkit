The Agentic Toolkit

A growing, curated collection of small, practical tools and agent skills for agentic engineering: the craft of shipping real, production software with AI coding agents.

Agentic engineering has six core elements, and this toolkit is organised by all of them. One new item lands here every newsletter issue.
